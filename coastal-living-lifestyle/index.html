The La Jolla Lifestyle

Living in La Jolla is about more than owning a beautiful home — it's about embracing a lifestyle that seamlessly blends natural beauty, cultural richness, and sophisticated leisure. The La Jolla coastal lifestyle is consistently ranked among the finest in America.

Beaches & Water Sports

La Jolla's coastline offers diverse beach experiences, from the protected waters of La Jolla Cove — perfect for snorkeling and kayaking — to the powerful surf breaks at Windansea Beach. Stand-up paddleboarding, scuba diving in the La Jolla Underwater Park, and surfing are daily activities for many residents.

Dining & Culinary Arts

La Jolla's restaurant scene rivals any coastal community in California. George's at the Cove offers rooftop ocean-view dining with award-winning cuisine, while the Village is home to dozens of internationally inspired restaurants, artisan cafes, and wine bars. The weekly farmers market brings the freshest local produce directly to the community.

Golf & Recreation

Torrey Pines Golf Course, home to the annual Farmers Insurance Open PGA Tour event, sits atop the cliffs overlooking the Pacific and offers one of the most spectacular golf experiences in the world. Additional courses in the area provide year-round golfing in San Diego's perfect climate.

Arts & Culture

The Museum of Contemporary Art San Diego, the La Jolla Playhouse (founded by Gregory Peck), and numerous galleries along Prospect Street make La Jolla a cultural destination. The La Jolla Music Society brings world-class performances to intimate venues throughout the year.
